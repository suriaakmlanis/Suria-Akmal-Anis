My Experience

In my first semester entering university, my first experience is when i am joining Student Representative Council. I've learn a lot from my senior to handle a program, from the simple one to the biggest event joining by the Rector and other Vips. Other than that, i learn how to overcome various problems voiced by students in the campus. Student Representative Council has opened my eyes to the weight of responsibility as a leader to protect and meet all the needs of the people under me. When i am entering my second semester i quit the council and start focusing on my training in SISPA.

I stay in SISPA for 2 years and half for rescue and medical training. I really gained a lot of experience and knowledge in SISPA untill last october, me and my fellow team was commisioned the rank of Young Lieutenant by Angkata Pertahanan Awam (APM). It is such an honor to be an officer in such a young age and got the chance to teach the next generation about APM.

I really gained a lot of experience during my service and i am really greatful for it.
